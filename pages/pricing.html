Frequently Asked Questions

How is pricing determined?

Pricing is based on your specific requirements including conversation volume, integrations needed, customization level, and support requirements. We provide transparent, custom quotes tailored to your needs.

Can I change plans later?

Yes, our flexible pricing model allows you to upgrade or modify your plan as your needs evolve. We work with you to ensure the best fit for your organization.

What's included in support?

Support includes technical assistance, implementation guidance, training, and ongoing optimization. Higher tiers include dedicated account managers and priority support.

Do you offer trials or demos?

Yes, we offer personalized demos and pilot programs to help you evaluate Emma AI's capabilities with your specific use cases before making a commitment.
