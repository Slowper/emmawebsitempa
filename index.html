About Us

The story behind Emma and the passionate team building the future of AI

The Basketball Game That Changed Everything

Every great innovation begins with a moment of frustration. Ours started on a basketball court.

🏀 The Game That Started It All

It was a typical Saturday morning pickup game. The court was alive with energy, players calling plays, the sound of sneakers on hardwood, and the satisfying swish of the net. But then it happened—a hard drive to the basket, a collision, and suddenly our founder was on the ground with a dislocated shoulder.

"The pain was intense, but what came next was even more frustrating..."

🏥 The Emergency Room Reality Check

What should have been a quick 30-minute visit to the emergency room turned into a 4-hour ordeal. Sitting in that waiting room, watching nurses frantically juggle patient files, seeing elderly patients struggle with complex appointment systems, and witnessing the chaos of manual scheduling—it was a wake-up call.

4+ Hours of waiting
15+ Patients struggling
1 Life-changing moment

💡 The Vision Takes Shape

In that moment of frustration, a simple question emerged: "What if there was a virtual assistant that could handle all this seamlessly?" Not just for healthcare, but for banking, education, and every industry where people struggle with complex systems.

📞 Phone-first approach - No computer skills required
🤖 AI-powered intelligence - Understands context and learns
🌍 Universal accessibility - Works for everyone, everywhere

🚀 Emma is Born

From that single moment of inspiration on the basketball court, Emma was born. An AI assistant designed to bridge the gap between complex technology and everyday people. Emma doesn't just schedule appointments—she understands, learns, and adapts to make healthcare, banking, and education more accessible to everyone.

Our Mission:

"To democratize AI technology and make it accessible to everyone, regardless of their technical background or digital literacy."

From Court to Code
